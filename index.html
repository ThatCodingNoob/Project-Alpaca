Respected Judges,

It is with immense pleasure that we present to you, Alpaca. Alpaca is a community-powered platform designed to provide individuals with a medium to acquire education based on a large variety of themes and subjects.

We would like to mention that this project has been created with hardcoded data. It is not a stable release, but it is a proof of concept that attempts to showcase how a fully functional variant of Alpaca may look like in the future.

We strongly urge you to go over the "README.pdf" file in our GitHub repo before reading further or alternatively, click here. Without doing so our concept will remain unclear.
